Tyler Insurance Filings users can access accounts using single sign-on

Safeguard your organization's information and simplify access with a more secure log-in option

Published: October 16, 2024

Tyler Insurance Filings supports enhanced account security through the availability of single sign-on (SSO) for companies that choose to opt in. SSO offers cloud-based authentication for authorized users to securely access their accounts from any location or device. Many organizations will be able to use internal authentication credentials to log in, eliminating the need to manage separate credentials for Tyler Insurance Filings.

SSO is a key part of our commitment to security, which is also reflected in our secure, online insurance filing services and cloud-based file storage.

"Maintaining a secure system is a top priority for Tyler Insurance Filings, and implementing single sign-on is a critical tool for managing account access," explained Helen Russell, General Manager of Tyler Insurance Filings. She added, "Companies using SSO benefit from enhanced identity verification, reducing the risk of unauthorized activity and ensuring that they know who is accessing their information or submitting filings on their behalf."

Tyler Insurance Filings can integrate with existing identity providers, including Federation SSO options like Entra ID (Azure), Google, and Okta. Alternatively, Tyler Insurance Filings can create a new Okta Workforce identity provider for company accounts upon request. Additionally, multi-factor authentication (MFA) is available as a part of Tyler Insurance Filings' SSO authentication process.

This past quarter, Tyler Insurance Filings successfully piloted a federated SSO integration and quickly established and validated account access in a few short weeks. Future SSO users can experience even faster setup as a result of work accomplished during this effort.

"Companies using SSO benefit from enhanced identify verification, reducing the risk of unauthorized activity."

- Helen Russell, Tyler Insurance Filings

Ready to reach your security goals with SSO?

Tyler Insurance Filings plans to roll out SSO for all accounts in the coming year. Companies can volunteer to be a leader in this transition by emailing support@tylerinsurancefilings.com to discuss next steps.
